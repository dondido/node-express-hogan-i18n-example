The Seasons Lodge Guest House is one of the finest three stars hotels on the east coast of Bulgaria. Situated on the edge of Albena this superb hotel is located in the old fishing village of Golden Fish, and is only a stone throw away from the beach. Being a privately owned and family run hotel allows us to make sure our residents and guests are treated the way we would like to be treated ourselves. We want you to sit back and relax. Indulge yourself with our facilities, and enjoy the warmth and hospitality offered to you at our hotel. We will try our very best to make sure your stay is an enjoyable one and we look forward to welcoming you back.
